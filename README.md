# CHAOGEJILATU
**Environmental Scientist** 路 ClimateAgriculture Impacts 路 Remote Sensing 路 GIS 路 Crop Modeling

I am **CHAOGEJILATU**, an environmental scientist focused on turning **Earth observation and geospatial data** into
**decision-ready insights** for climate risk and agricultural resilience.  
Currently based in **Tsukuba, Japan**, I work at the **National Agriculture and Food Research Organization (NARO)**.

---

##  RESEARCH FOCUS
- **Climate change & food security** (risk, impacts, resilience)
- **Land use / land cover change** and environmental sustainability
- **Remote sensing  GIS  statistical modeling** (reproducible analytics)
- **International collaboration** across East Asia and global networks
